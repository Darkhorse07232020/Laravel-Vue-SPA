How to convert to gray scale and washed out for image

IrfanView = Software

With Color Image:
(Toolbar) --> Image --> Color Corrections
Contrast = 50
Click "OK"
(Toolbar) --> Image --> Convert to Gracyscale
(Toolbar) --> Image --> Color Corrections
Brightness Range =  100 to 150
Contrast Range= 0 to 50
To Test Click "Apply to original"
Click "OK"
Save
